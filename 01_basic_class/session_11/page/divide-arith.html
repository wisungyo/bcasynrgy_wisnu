Division Arithmetic

Take a look at this background color
 

First of all, enter two numbers into first and second column.

Hit Process button to procced

Take a look at the third column. By the default the color is grey. It will change the color according to the process result.

The color will turn to green if the result is even, turn yellow if odd, and turn to blue if the result is zero.

The last column will parse you the result from numbering format into word format.
